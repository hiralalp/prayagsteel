Brass Specifications

Brass is an alloy made from copper and zinc, known for its excellent corrosion resistance, machinability, and attractive gold-like appearance. It is widely used in applications like plumbing, electrical components, musical instruments, and decorative hardware. Brass is available in various grades, including C260, C280, and C360, with options for sheets, plates, rods, tubes, and fittings. Its durability, low friction, and antimicrobial properties make it a versatile material suitable for demanding environments.
