Wide Range Of Sheets , Plates & Coil

Our forged fittings are crafted for high-pressure and high-temperature applications, available in types like elbows, tees, couplings, unions, plugs, bushings, and caps. We manufacture them in materials such as stainless steel, carbon steel, alloy steel, and high nickel alloys, adhering to standards like ASME/ANSI B16.11, with sizes ranging from 1/8” to 4” and pressure ratings of 2000#, 3000#, 6000#, and 9000#. These fittings come in socket weld and threaded connections, undergo strict quality testing, and are supplied with certifications like EN 10204 3.1/3.2 and NACE MR 01-75 compliance.
