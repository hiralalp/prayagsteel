Wide Range Of Buttweld Fittings

Our buttweld fittings are available in a variety of types, including elbows, tees, reducers, caps, stub ends, and crosses, made from materials like stainless steel, carbon steel, alloy steel, high nickel alloys, and copper alloys. They comply with standards such as ASME/ANSI B16.9 and B16.28, with sizes ranging from ½” NB to 48” NB and wall thicknesses from SCH 5 to SCH XXS. Designed for high performance, they come with beveled ends, support pressure ratings up to 2500#, and undergo rigorous testing with certifications like EN 10204 3.1/3.2 and NACE MR 01-75 compliance.
